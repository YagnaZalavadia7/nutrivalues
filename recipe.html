Crostini

Home  Popular Recipe  Nutri Values 

Description

Crostini are an Italian appetizer consisting of small slices of grilled or toasted bread and toppings. This appetizer is one of my favorite and quick recipe of the food book list. It is healthy and easy to make which altogether meets the daily requirements of the necessary diet for the body. Don't forget that healthy stuffs are tasty too!!.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white burger buns
  • 2 finely chopped capsicums
  • 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise
  • 1 bowl of grated cheese
  • 1 cup boiled sweet corn
  • 6-7 black
  • 2 spoon of oil
  • herbs of your choice
  • A butter knife

Directions

  1. Cut down the capsicums of your choice.
  2. With the butter knife, cut the burger buns into half.
  3. Toast the halfed burger buns on both the sides.
  4. In a pan add 1 tbsp of oil and saute chopped capsicums for a minute or two.
  5. Mix mayonnaise and grated cheese with the herbs like oregano and chilli flakes.
  6. Apply the generous mixer of mayonnaise and cheese on the slices of the buns.
  7. Decorate with sweet corns and olives.
  8. Enjoy the Crostini!
